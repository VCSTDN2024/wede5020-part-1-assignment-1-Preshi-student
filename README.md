Project Overview 
The project is based on creating a website about a target organisation whether a non-profit organisation, a small business, or even a retail store. I choose to do a non-profit organisation whereby 
I decided to create a website project about an orphange.  The orphange is called "Kindroot Haven" and is based on providing a safe and healthy environment for orphans and abadoned children. 

We target all orphans and abadoned children who are thrown away and abused where we take them in and support them. Once they are in Kindroot Haven's hands, they are given a safe environment to grow in, 
whereby they have access to education and emotional support.  

Our vision for the Project 
Our vision as Kind Root Haven is to nurture resilient, and compassionate children to grow into empowered adults that then get ready to shape their communities with kindness, courage, and purpose. 

Our Mission for the Project 
Our mission is to provide a safe, nurtured environment where orphans and vulnerable children will receive special childcare, emotional support and even have access to education.  This will empower these children to heal, grow and even thrive within a loving community. 

Our Target Audience 
Our target market is to individuals who are willing to give back such as working professionals, retirees, and parents. 
Also, organisations that are involved in charity in order to support child welfare. 
Our target market is also to community members who can donate small but have consistent support such as clothing, food, tutoring.   

What are our goals and objectives for this project?

Goals

One of our goals is to provide holistic care to the orphans such as nutrition, education, and emotional support. 
To empower children to become confident, compassionate growing into empowering individuals. 
We also aim to foster community involvement through volunteer programs, partnerships, and awareness campaigns. 
We aim to promote long-term development by offering access to education, life skills, and mentorship. 
We aim to ensure sustainability through transparent operations, and strategic growth. 

Objectives

We aim to publish a safe and nurturing residential fertility with trained caregivers and staff that aims to support these children. 
To implement educational programs that include tutoring, school enrolment, and helping them to learn creative activities. 
We aim to provide social and psychological support through counselling, group therapy, and wellness workshops. 
We aim to launch community outreach initiatives to raise awareness and to reduce stigma around child vulnerability. 
We aim to partner with local schools, clinics, and NGOs to expand access to resources and services. 
We plan to be able to monitor the progress through regular evaluations, child development tracking

•	Website traffic- This tracks how many people visit the website. 
•	Donation Conversions – This measures how many visitors make a financial contribution on the website. 
•	Volunteer signups – This counts how many users apply to volunteer through our website. 
•	Newsletter subscriptions – This counts how many users sign up for the updates. 
•	Time spent on site – This indicates how engaging the content is. 
•	Social media shares – This often reflects how users share the website’s content externally. 
•	Event Registrations - This tracks sign-ups for Kind Root Haven’s community events.
